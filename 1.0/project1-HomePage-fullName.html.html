WORDLE

The rules of wordle:

Wordle is a daily word game where you get six tries to guess a secret five-letter word. After each guess, tiles turn green if a letter is correct and in the right spot, yellow if the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, and gray if the letter isn't in the word at all. The game provides clues with each guess, and if you guess the word within six tries, you win.
